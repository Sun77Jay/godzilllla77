About Me Section

Jay Wilson aka JaySun is a 45 year old California native that currently resides in Camden, NJ. since 2003. I wanted to create a website for the cratediggas that still listen to records, but they're not as internet savvy as other people who shop online to purchase records from a good quality hip-hop website or websites. Making a webpage, that provide cratediggas links to a specific online hip-hop record store of their choice to purchase records, is beneficial for them. Purchasing hip-hop records online in some instances is more convenient than physically going and searching for a certain record in your area. It saves time and gas money, but the drawback though for some cratediggas is waiting for the record or records to arrive via mail. I myself can be a little impatient when waiting for a record to arrive, but at least I know that a specific hip-hop record that I'm looking for is available online when it's not available in the area I reside at.
